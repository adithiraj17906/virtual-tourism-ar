Our Vision

At ARound the world, we're revolutionizing the way people experience travel through cutting-edge augmented reality technology. Our mission is to break down geographical barriers and make immersive travel experiences accessible to everyone.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, ARound the world emerged from a simple yet powerful idea: what if we could bring the world's most breathtaking destinations directly to people's homes? Our team of travel enthusiasts and tech innovators came together to create an unprecedented AR travel experience platform.
